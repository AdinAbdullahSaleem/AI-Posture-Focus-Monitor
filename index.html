How to use:
  1. Click Start Camera and grant camera permission.
  2. Sit in your ideal posture, looking at the screen.
  3. Click Calibrate Posture — the AI saves your baseline.
  4. If bad posture is sustained beyond the alarm delay, a siren sounds.
  5. Fix your posture to silence it. Adjust sensitivity and delay in Settings.

100% private — all AI processing runs locally in your browser. No video data is transmitted.
